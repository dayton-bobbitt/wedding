Fun things to do

Not from the Raleigh area? Here are some great places to eat and fun ways to pass the time.

Farmers' Market

Website | Directions

A great place to buy produce grown by local farmers, as well as multiple restaurants where you can eat amazing food.

The Flea Market

Website | Directions

If you are looking for something to do before the wedding on Saturday or afterwards on Sunday, the Flea Market is a great place to purchase some really cool items for a great price.

Umstead Park

Website | Directions

One of North Carolina's State Parks that spans all the way from Raleigh to Cary. Laura and I have enjoyed many hikes and long runs at this location.

Beasley's Chicken and Honey

Website | Directions

Chicken and waffles often get a weird look when people first hear about the combination but we love them and Beasley's is one of the best places to get it.

Goodberry's

Website | Directions

Our favorite place to get a cup of ice cream and local to the Raleigh/Durham/Cary area.

N.C. Museum of Art

Website | Directions

A great art museum and the location of our wedding! The indoor galaries are a great place to visit on a hot day, and there are multiple greenways if you are in the mood for a long walk or run.

N.C. Museum of History

Website | Directions

N.C. Museum of Natural Sciences

Website | Directions

Laura and I both made frequent visits to these museums when we were younger, and they are still a fun way to spend an afternoon in Downtown Raleigh.

Crabtree Valley Mall

The best mall in North Carolina with lots of good shops and restaurants.

North Hills Mall
